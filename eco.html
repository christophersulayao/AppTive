EcoPCO D-X

EcoPCO D-X Eco PCO D-X Dust is a ready-to-use, all-natural, organic insecticide dust with the botanical active ingredients Phenethyl Propionate and Pyrethrins. The active ingredient is 2-Phenthyl Propionate.

2-Phenthyl Propionate acts as an octopamine blocker for target pests by disrupting the neuro transmitter function in target insects and causing a breakdown in the insect's central nervous system.

    Target Pests:
  • Ants
  • Bed Bugs
  • Carpet Beetles
  • Centipedes
  • Cockroaches
  • Spiders
  • Stinging Insects
