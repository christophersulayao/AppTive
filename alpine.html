Alpine WSG (water-soluble granule) is a non-pyrethroid, non-repellent broad-spectrum insecticide that uses the active ingredient Dinotefuran. The active ingredient is Dinotefuran.

Dinotefuran's mechanism of action involves disruption of the insect's nervous system by inhibiting nicotinic acetylcholine receptors.

    Target Pests:
  • Ants (Argetnine,carpenter, harvester, odorous, Red imported, south fire)
  • Bed Bugs
  • Centipedes
  • Crickets
  • Earwigs
  • Fleas
  • German Cockroaches
  • Millipedes
  • Mosquitos
  • Paper Wasps
  • Pill Bugs
  • Sow Bugs
  • Spiders(excluding brown recluse)
  • Yellow Jackets
